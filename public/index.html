🌤️ Smart tips for June on Sylt

  • 🌊 North Sea water is brisk in early June (~14–16 °C) — perfect for wading, brisk for swimming.
  • 💨 Sylt is windy year-round — a packable windbreaker beats an umbrella on the dunes.
  • 🧴 Long beach days on open sand = strong reflection. Pack high-SPF and lip balm.
  • 👟 Wooden boardwalks at Wanderdünen and stony stretches at Odde — bring decent walking shoes.
  • 🐟 Budget €5–8 for Gosch fish buns at Lister Hafen — a Sylt ritual.
  • 🚌 Screenshot SVG Line 1 / 2 / 3 last-departure times before each beach block.
