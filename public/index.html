Base yourselves in Travemünde or Timmendorfer Strand and work the bay's most beautiful resort coast — from the cruise-ship horizon at Travemünde to the wild, meadow-backed sands of Sierksdorf. This guide stitches every beach, train, break and euro into one easy rhythm.

🌤️ Smart tips for June

  • 🌊 Baltic water is brisk in early June (~15–17 °C) — great for wading, bracing for swimming.
  • 🧴 Long beach days = strong reflection. Pack high-SPF and a light cover-up.
  • 👟 The cliff and natural beaches (Brodten, Sierksdorf) can be stony — water shoes help.
  • 🌧️ Rain likely on Jun 5 — a packable rain jacket beats a bulky umbrella in the wind.
  • 💨 Sea breeze drops the "feels-like" temp fast — bring a warm layer for evenings.
  • ⛴️ The Priwall ferry runs every few minutes — keep small change handy just in case.
