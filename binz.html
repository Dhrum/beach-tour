3. Comprehensive Beach Directory

1. Prora Beach
Location: Prora | Access: RE9 Train / Bus 20[cite: 2]
Expansive, wide sandy terrain running alongside historic pine groves. Remarkably uncrowded in the morning, making it optimal for long group walks on dry sand right by the water line[cite: 2].
2. Binzer Strand (Main Beach)
Location: Central Binz | Access: Short walk from center[cite: 2]
The primary 5-kilometer white-sand resort beach[cite: 2]. Exceptionally flat sea floor sloping out into the Baltic Sea, perfectly customized for waist-deep wading without dealing with crashing waves.
3. Südstrand Binz
Location: Southern Binz | Access: Walk via Promenade[cite: 2]
A transition zone heading away from the busy village center[cite: 2]. Features a highly relaxed environment, fewer crowds, and optimal spaces to spread out large picnic blankets directly on the sand.
4. Fischerstrand
Location: Mid Binz | Access: Walk via Promenade[cite: 2]
A small, historically rich beach stretch where local fishing boats still operate[cite: 2]. Offers a rustic maritime atmosphere with a clean shoreline to sit down and rest near the historic vessels.
5. Main Pier Beach (Nordstrand Sellin)
Location: Sellin | Access: Bus 20 from Binz hub[cite: 2]
Home to the world-famous 394-meter wooden pier structure and its over-water restaurant pavilion[cite: 1, 2]. High cliffs frame the background, providing a dramatic landscape to sit on the stone seawall.
6. Südstrand Sellin
Location: South Sellin | Access: Walkway via cliff side[cite: 2]
A more sheltered, sunny beach bay tucked south of the main pier cliffs[cite: 2]. Extremely calm waters with a gradual incline, making it very comfortable to walk in half-body water.
7. Baabe Beach
Location: Baabe | Access: Bus 20 or Walk from Sellin[cite: 2]
A flawless, exceptionally wide white-sand beach known for its highly regulated clean shallow waters[cite: 2]. Perfect transition point to set up a mid-day resting spot directly on the fine sand.
8. Göhren Nordstrand (North Beach)
Location: Göhren | Access: Bus 20 Terminus[cite: 2]
A lively seaside destination featuring a busy beach promenade, manicured lawns, and its own short pier[cite: 2]. The water here is crystal clear and remains shallow quite far from the shoreline.
9. Südstrand Göhren (South Beach)
Location: South Göhren Peninsula | Access: Walk via town ridge[cite: 2]
A completely natural, wild beach landscape featuring a unique mixture of fine sand and smooth coastal stones[cite: 2]. Gives the group an isolated, rugged view of the sea away from any holiday crowds.
